Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our placement process

When should I register for placements?

Students should register at the beginning of their final year. Early registration ensures you don't miss any opportunities and gives you time to prepare your profile and documents.

What documents do I need for registration?

You'll need academic transcripts, resume, passport-size photograph, identity proof, and any relevant certificates. All documents should be scanned and uploaded in the specified format.

How does the placement process work?

The process involves registration, eligibility screening, pre-placement talks, aptitude/technical tests, group discussions, interviews, and finally offer letters. Each step is clearly communicated to all eligible students.

Can I apply to multiple companies?

Yes, you can apply to multiple companies. However, once you accept an offer, you're typically bound by that commitment. We encourage students to be selective and apply to companies that align with their career goals.

What support does the placement cell provide?

We provide career counseling, resume review, mock interviews, skill workshops, pre-placement talks coordination, interview scheduling, and post-placement support. Our team is available to assist you throughout the process.

The average package is ‚Çπ12 LPA, with packages ranging from ‚Çπ8 LPA to ‚Çπ28 LPA depending on the company, role, and student's profile. Packages vary across different industries and specializations.
